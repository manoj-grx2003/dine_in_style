Our Story

Preserving Nepal's culinary heritage through generations of authentic flavors

A Taste of Himalayan Tradition

Namaste Restaurant was established in 2010 by Mr. Manoj Gurung, who was driven to introduce the world to Nepal's diverse culinary customs. From the slow-cooked tenderness of our Khasi Ko Masu (goat curry) to the delicate balance of spices in our signature Momo dumplings, our journey started in a small kitchen in Kathmandu where generations-old recipes were refined.

Today, we are the top destination in Kathmandu for real Nepali food, where each dish narrates tales of family kitchens, mountain villages, and historic spice routes. In order to guarantee that every bite contains the genuine textures and scents that characterize Himalayan cooking, our chefs continue to grind spices by hand using a traditional silauto (mortar) and lohta (pestle).

What really sets our food apart are the traditional methods we use, like the clay pots that simmer our Dal Bhat to a creamy richness, the charcoal-fired tandoors that give our Sekuwa its smoky perfection, and the careful hand-folding that turns our Momo dumplings into culinary masterpieces.

Our Culinary Philosophy

Time-Tested Recipes

Every dish on our menu is derived from family cookbooks that have been passed down through the generations, maintaining the flavors that have been enjoyed in Nepali homes for centuries. For example, the fermentation method used in Himalayan villages is exactly the same as our Gundruk soup recipe.

Himalayan Ingredients

We source directly from Nepal's mountain valleys and terai farms, including the rare Timur berries that give our chutneys their unique citrusy punch, the fiery chillies from Palpa, and the fragrant jira (cumin) from Dang. Our rice, which is renowned for having the ideal texture, is grown in the lush fields of Janakpur.

Artisan Techniques

Our chefs spend years honing traditional techniques, such as making the ideal hand-pulled roti, fermenting our Sel roti for precisely 72 hours, and setting the charcoal temperature that gives our Tandoori dishes their legendary status. These abilities cannot be automated or hurried.
